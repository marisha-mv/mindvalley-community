Questions

Before you apply.

What is the AI Guild?+

A private, application-only network for people building with AI. Not a course — a room of founders, operators and creators, kept on the frontier through weekly sessions, expert drops, mastermind pods, tool deals and demo days.

Who gets in?+

Founders, operators, creators and rising AI voices who are actively building. Everyone applies — being qualified isn't enough. We vet every applicant; the bar is what makes the room worth being in.

How does the application work?+

Apply for free. A member of our team reviews your application personally, and we'll reach out for a short conversation. AI Mastery graduates are fast-tracked within ~24 hours.

What does membership include?+

Weekly frontier sessions, a monthly marquee expert, mastermind pods and project groups, the AI tools wall (20–30% off), demo-day access, talent matching, and the full network of founding faces.

How much does it cost?+

$5,000 a year. AI Mastery graduates who qualify pay $2,500 for their first year, then renew at full price. Founding-cohort pricing is locked for life.

What if it's not for me?+

You're covered by our 30-day promise — if the Guild isn't the most valuable room you belong to, we'll refund you in full.
