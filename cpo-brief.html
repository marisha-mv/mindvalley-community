The flow

From "Apply" to inside the room

The end-to-end path. Note where it's automated vs. a human, and the tool behind each step.

1

Apply — no money taken live page

Member applies on the public page. Free. The form must actually capture — right now it's a prototype.

Build: wire the application form → Airtable (the review CRM). ~half a day.
2

Review & vetting build (light)

A concierge reviews each application in Airtable: Approve / Reject / Needs-call. Mastery grads flagged for the 24h fast-track. Borderline/external → short call (Calendly).

Build: an Airtable view + simple status workflow + canned accept/reject emails. Human in the loop — no AI decisioning in v1.
3

Payment link build

On approval, member gets a payment link. $5,000/yr (or $2,500 first-year for Mastery grads). Payment is the trigger for access.

Build: Stripe payment link (or MV checkout) per tier. Decide: charge via MV commerce or standalone Stripe? (open debate)
4

Access to FinerMinds build (automation)

Payment success → member is invited into the AI Guild space on FinerMinds.

Use + build: FM email/CSV invite exists; automate the "paid → invite" trigger (Stripe webhook → FM invite via API/Zapier). For v1 this can even be manual daily.
5

Onboarding — the first session build · the one true gap

The make-or-break moment. New member is routed to: complete profile + set a goal → meet the AI Guild Master → join one pod → post a first intro → get matched to 3 people.

Build: a "Start Here" onboarding flow. MVP-light = a pinned checklist + auto-DM + "Members to Meet" intros. This is the single most important thing to build.

The architecture

FinerMinds × MV platform × BlinkLife

Three systems, three jobs. Keeping them clean is what makes the MVP buildable.

FinerMinds
The home
  • Where the community lives
  • Space, feed, pods, events, chat
  • Profiles, library, member data
  • Hosts the AI Guild Master
MV Platform
Commerce & identity
  • Sells the Guild (checkout / Stripe)
  • Verifies AI Mastery grads → fast-track + discount
  • Membership entitlement
  • Open: SSO into FM, or separate FM login?
BlinkLife
The AI brain
  • Powers the AI Guild Master
  • Trained on program transcripts + member context
  • Runs accountability + content suggestions
  • Open: lives inside FM, or linked tool?
My recommendation

The MVP in one line

Ship the room on FinerMinds-as-is + three new builds: commerce flow, week-one onboarding, and a "lite" AI Guild Master.

Run everything else manually (challenges, vetting, content). Prove that paid members activate in week one and stay. Then build the gamification, marketplace and full AI/dashboard once retention is proven.

Deliberately cut from v1: native challenges, Spark/ranks, progress cards, monthly recap, demand map, affiliate marketplace, full custom GM dashboard, SSO. None of them are needed to prove the core loop.

Let's debate

The open questions

  • Commerce: charge through MV checkout, or a standalone Stripe link for speed in v1?
  • Access: SSO from MV into FinerMinds, or provision a separate FM account on payment? (Manual invite is fine for v1.)
  • AI Guild Master in v1? It's the moat but the heaviest build. Ship a "lite" version (transcript Q&A + accountability nudges) now, or hold it to v1.1 and run human-concierge-heavy at launch?
  • BlinkLife integration: does it live inside FinerMinds, or as a linked tool members open alongside? What's the real integration effort?
  • GM dashboard: thin Airtable/Retool view over FM stats for v1, or invest in a custom build now?
  • Challenges: agree they're manual in v1, or is the native feature a must-have for the heartbeat?
  • Who runs it at launch: the human Guild Master + the concierge reviewing applications — who are they?
